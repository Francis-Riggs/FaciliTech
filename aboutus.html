About FaciliTech

FaciliTech Software Solutions is a software company dedicated to providing the most effective software tools for facilities management. Our goal at FaciliTech is to help facilities professionals succeed by providing simple, flexible, and powerful software solutions for managing all aspects of building maintenance. Because we value flexibility and ease of use, we specialize in cloud-based web applications that can be used from any device.
