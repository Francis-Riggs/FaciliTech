Frequently Asked Questions

  • Question: What is a service request system, and how can it benefit my maintenance department?

    Answer: A service request system is software used for submitting and managing service requests (or work orders) submitted by customers. Service request systems make it far easier for facilities professionals to receive and organize service requests than other methods (e.g., direct emails), which is why most facilities departments rely on service request management systems to handle work orders.

  • Question: How do I decide what service request system is right for me?

    Answer: Some of the key factors for selecting a service request system are simplicity, flexibility, and accessibility. Work order systems are used by a wide variety of people, so it's important that the software is simple and easy to use. Work order systems should also be flexible enough to accommodate varying department sizes, and they should be accessible from as many devices as possible.
